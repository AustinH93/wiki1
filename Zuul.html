Throughout the ages of uncertainty, Gilthosians, Vinidrans and Saikhrans alike have found solace in the majesty of Zuul.

Physical characteristics

Zuul is estimated to have formed some 5.3 billion years ago when large clouds of Hydrogen, Helium and other lighter trace elements were ejected from the supernova of Enetas.  This gas and dust coalesced into a sphere which is approximately 1,412,834 miles in diameter.  It's surface glows a faint yellow-white and it's surface temperature averages 12,335oF (6,835oC).
