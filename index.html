The System of Zuul

Zuul is a star system in the Trinitus cluster.  It is composed of:

  • Zuul - The central star.
  • Heltens - The closest planet to Zuul.
  • Milar - The second planet from Zuul.
  • Gilthos - The third planet from Zuul.
  • Vinidras - The fourth planet from Zuul.
  • Celos - The fifth planet from Zuul.
  • Saikhra - The sixth planet from Zuul.
